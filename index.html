George Larkwright

Hello, I’m George, and I’m a writer and game designer from London, UK.

I make games that use play to satirise the society we live in, that tell stories in new and dynamic ways, and that strive to subvert the status quo, sparking discussion and debate. I also enjoy writing for the stage and screen.

Last year I produced Experiments in Play, a showcase of inventive and experimental playful experiences at the intersection of games, interactive design, and creative technology. I'm currently working on the world's smallest escape room.

I write about the development of my projects in my blog - you can find that here. You can find out more about other things I’ve worked on here. If you want to chat about some (or all) of the above, free to get in touch with me over on Twitter, or by email, using the links below.


Also, my itch.io page can be found here if you want a one-stop shop for all currently playable work.
