George Larkwright

Hello, I’m George, and I’m a writer and game designer from London, UK.

I make games that use play to satirise the society we live in, that tell stories in new and dynamic ways, and that strive to subvert the status quo, sparking discussion and debate. I also enjoy writing humorous stage plays and interactive fiction.

I’m currently working on a trio of short games examining the negative impact of social media on society, as well as an escape room set in a small wooden crate. I write about the development process of these projects in my blog - you can find that here. You can find out more about other things I’ve worked on here.

Otherwise, if you want to chat about some (or all) of the above, please get in touch with me over on Twitter or on the good ol’ emails using the links below.
