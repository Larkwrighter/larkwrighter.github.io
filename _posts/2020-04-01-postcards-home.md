<b>Postcards Home</b> is a relaxing experience about exploring the world, staying in touch with family, and making the most of the time we have. Fly across Europe in a paper aeroplane, stopping off in cities to send postcards home.

The game was developed at a time when travel was impossible due to the COVID-19 pandemic. At a time of such uncertainty, I wanted to make something that focused on human connection overcoming distance.

Postcards Home was developed over the course of a week as part of the 8 Bits to Infinity 'VimJam' game jam, using <b>Construct 3</b>, with sound design by Molly Linfield, and photos sourced from the <a href="https://ccsearch-dev.creativecommons.org/">Creative Commons Search</a>.
